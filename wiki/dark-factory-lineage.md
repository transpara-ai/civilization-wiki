# Dark Factory Lineage

Dark Factory Lineage is the rule that [[dark-factory]] is preserved as the
formative production doctrine inside the larger Civilization umbrella. The
Stage 0 source states the relationship plainly:

```text
Civilization is the whole organism.
Dark Factory is a formative production doctrine and historical lineage.
```

This is not a rename. Stage 0 specifically did not move or rename
`docs/dark-factory`. The lineage article exists so future migration work can
change naming without erasing citations, accepted baselines, or the historical
meaning of Dark Factory.

## Lineage carried forward

The Stage 0 lineage index identifies these copied or indexed layers:

| Layer | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Seed | Orientation, motive, goal, approach, and early civic-role framing. |
| Phase record | Kernel, initial audit, and authority-gated side-effect doctrine. |
| v3 and v3.8 | Archived and accepted pre-v3.9 lineage. |
| [[v3-9]] | Accepted operative baseline. |
| [[v4-0]] | Candidate doctrine shift: the factory builds the factory. |
| Reunification | The workstream that re-centers the living Civilization inside the factory membrane. |
| Landscape | The May 2026 investigation that shaped the v3.9 delta. |

## Migration rule

Any later transition from `dark-factory` naming to `civilization` naming has to
preserve old URLs or redirects, accepted v3.9/v4.0 provenance, historical
citations, and the identity of Dark Factory as lineage rather than discarded
terminology.

The wiki follows that rule now: Dark Factory remains a first-class article, and
this lineage article explains its place inside the broader institutional
substrate.
